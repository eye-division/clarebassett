Clare has been working for over thirty years creating lithographs, screenprints and oil paintings of monumental female figures. Her  prints are limited editions and each one is hand-printed and then hand-coloured in her studios. She works in London and Gloucestershire.  More recently she has been creating work by responding to live dance movement. This rapid, gestural, abstract  work is an interesting foil to the studied figurative pieces.
